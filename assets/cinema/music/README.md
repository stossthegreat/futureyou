# Cinematic Music Assets

This folder should contain background music for the Future-You cinematic experience.

## Required Files:

1. **hope.mp3** - Uplifting, inspirational music for:
   - Chapter I (The Call)
   - Chapter IV (The Mentor)  
   - Chapter VI (The Path)

2. **conflict.mp3** - Tense, introspective music for:
   - Chapter II (The Conflict)
   - Chapter III (The Mirror)

3. **promise.mp3** - Powerful, climactic music for:
   - Chapter V (The Task)
   - Chapter VII (The Promise)

## Recommendations:

- **Duration:** 3-5 minutes per track (will loop)
- **Format:** MP3, 128-192 kbps
- **Style:** Cinematic, ambient, instrumental
- **Volume:** Normalized to prevent clipping

## Where to Find Music:

- **Free:** YouTube Audio Library, Incompetech, Free Music Archive
- **Paid:** Epidemic Sound, Artlist, AudioJungle
- **AI Generated:** Soundraw, Mubert, AIVA

## Note:

The app will work without music files (music will simply not play). Add music files here and they will automatically be used.
